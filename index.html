Buying a home is emotional. That's the problem.

You fall in love with the bay window. You picture your kids in the garden. You start planning the renovation before you've even checked the BER cert.

We're the clear-headed friend who walks through the property with you and asks the questions you forgot to ask. Not to talk you out of it — but to make sure you're walking in with your eyes open.

We don't just flag problems. Sometimes a property has far more potential than you think — a layout that could be opened up, a renovation that's more feasible than it looks, a home that actually fits your life better than you realised.

What we help with

  • Red flags — structural concerns, planning risks, costly surprises
  • Layout & renovation potential — what could change, what can't
  • Lifestyle fit — does this home actually suit your needs?
  • The right questions — what to ask the agent, the vendor, your solicitor
  • Value perspective — is the price aligned with what you're getting?
  • Confidence — whether you proceed or walk away, you'll know why

How it works

  1. 01
